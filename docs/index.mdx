Adjacent News features a data platform for various prediction markets, along with the ability to fetch realted news and related markets. 

Markets and news can also be read "in context" with our extension and RSS reader. 

Note that that /markets/{index} endpoint is currentely down until we transition to v2 of the API.

You can view a trading interface at [trade.adj.news](https://trade.adj.news)

> The charting technology is provided by TradingView. Keep track the most important cryptos like bitcoin usd, eth usd and much more.
